THE AFRICAN BASKETBALL ACADEMY.

The African Basketball Academy is an elite basketball training center in Nigeria for the top male and female prospects from throughout Africa and the first of its kind on the continent.

The academy is a partnership between the NBA and SEED Project (Sports for Education and Economic Development), a non-profit organization based in Lagos, Nigeria.

The academy also uses basketball as a platform to engage youth in academic, athletic and leadership programs.
